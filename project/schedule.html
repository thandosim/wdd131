CHICKEN AND SPINACH SELF-RELIANCE PROGRAM

HOME SCHEDULE REGISTER

It is imperative to the success of the program that participants use their own resources to build a chicken coop and to prepare their land for planting. It is after all a Self-Reliance program and empowers families to take care of their needs. Failies who are able to meet These requirements by the rollout dates are provided with the materials. They then are expected to be ready to return 10 chickens at the end of the 3 month period so that the next batch of families can be supplied. Families who may miss one rollot stage can continue to prepare and be ready for the next one as materials are issued every 3 months to new families.
